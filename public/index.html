Lyrica

Lyrica is an app that brings your music to life with perfectly synced lyrics. Dive into a unique musical journey where every song reveals its story, one lyric at a time. In addition to featuring lyrics powered by the lrclib library, Lyrica also includes Malagasy songs from the renowned Tononkira collection. Enjoy a rich and authentic experience that combines precise lyric synchronization with the cultural diversity of Malagasy music.
